    cubx3D : Campus of University of BordeauX in 3D

    Collaborative project of students of the master of sciences in Bioinformatics of Bordeaux, France. The aim of this project is to visualize in 3D the campus Science & Technologies of the university of Bordeaux using HTML5, CSS3, and WebGL.
